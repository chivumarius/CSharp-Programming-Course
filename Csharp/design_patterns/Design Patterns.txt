

	Design Patterns





  Book (24 oct 1994):

    • Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software
      or Gang of Four (GoF)



  What is a design pattern and who are the Gang of Four?

    • A design pattern is a general, reusable solution to a commonly occuring problem in software engineering.



  There are 3 types of design patterns patterns (that contain 23 design patterns):

 	1. Creational - used to create objects 
	2. Structural - used for relationships between objects
	3. Behavioral - used to define how objects interact
